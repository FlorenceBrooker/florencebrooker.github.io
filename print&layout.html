Museum Brochure Design

This project involved choosing a local museum and designing a brochure to go with the exhibition. I chose to create an interactive brochure aimed at children to help guide them through the museum. This is the brochure cover design. As you can see, the brochure is a clear reflection of the mood board above, showing that I am able to maintain consistency in visuals and aesthetics throughout the design process.
